August #1 Bye Bye Paris !

It is time to say goodbye to Paris.

This past weeks, I have been trying to go to all the places I love: walking near the Seine, in the little streets of Montmartre, from l’Arc de Triomphe to the Tour Eiffel, enjoying and capturing the last memories.
I am spending the last moments with my family and friends, celebrating, laughing and of course eating the last baguette, croissant...very stereotypical but true.
My suitcase is almost done. It is difficult to try to pack for a whole year. There are probably several things I have not anticipated, but this is part of the adventure.
I am very excited to leave !

See you next time in Singapore!
